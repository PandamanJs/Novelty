## ðŸš€ Quick Start

### 1. AI Writing Assistant

**What it does**: Analyzes your writing and provides real-time suggestions

**How to use**:
1. Start writing in the editor
2. AI automatically analyzes (every ~1.5 seconds)
3. Click "AI Assistant" in the sidebar
4. Select writing profile (Genre, Audience)
5. Review suggestions in tabs: Overview, Grammar, Words, Style, Advanced
6. Click "Re-analyze" to update suggestions anytime

**Best for**: Grammar checking, style improvement, vocabulary enhancement

---

### 2. Plot & Character Assistant

**What it does**: Guides story structure and character development

**How to use**:
1. Click "Plot & Character" in sidebar
2. Choose a tab:
   - **Plot**: See 3-Act structure and story beats
   - **Characters**: Add characters and define their arcs
   - **Tension**: View story tension curve and pacing tips
   - **Tips**: Get recommendations based on your word count

**Best for**: Planning story structure, developing character arcs, pacing

---

### 3. Context-Aware Rewriter

**What it does**: Generates alternative phrasings in different styles

**How to use**:
1. Click "Rewrite & Enhance" in sidebar
2. Select a rewrite style (Natural, Concise, Descriptive, etc.)
3. Paste/type text to rewrite in the textarea
4. Click "Generate Options"
5. Choose from 5 alternative versions
6. Copy any option to clipboard

**Best for**: Improving prose, varying sentence structure, finding stronger words

## ðŸ“ˆ Metrics Explained

### Flesch Grade Level
- **0-6**: Easy (children's book)
- **6-9**: Simple (general audience)
- **9-12**: Standard (most fiction)
- **12-14**: Difficult (academic)
- **14+**: Very difficult (technical)

### Readability
- **Color indicators**: Green (good), Yellow (fair), Red (needs work)
- **Paragraph score**: How well-structured your paragraph is
- **Sentence variety**: Mix of short and long sentences

### Word Frequency
- **Repetition alerts**: Same word appears 3+ times
- **Synonym suggestions**: Alternatives for overused words
- **Vocabulary diversity**: Measure of word variation
